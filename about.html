Who We Are

At KP Gifting and KP Fashion, we believe that expressing yourself through custom apparel shouldn't break the bank. Based in the East Hartford, CT area, we started this business with a simple, core mission: to provide high-quality, personalized clothing and gifts at truly affordable prices.

Whether you are designing a single hoodie for a friend's birthday, outfitting your entire startup team, or creating memorable personalized gifts, we treat every order with the same level of care and precision.

We pride ourselves on lightning-fast turnarounds, with standard orders often ready within a single day. Our local roots mean we care deeply about our community and the quality of the products we hand over to our neighbors.

Thank you for choosing us to bring your imagination to life.
